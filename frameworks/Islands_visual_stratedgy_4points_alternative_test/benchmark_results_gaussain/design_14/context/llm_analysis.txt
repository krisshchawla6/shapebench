    - There is a noticeable "pinch" between the leading edge and the mid-body, creating a non-convex, boomerang-like profile.

- **Flow Field Observation**:

    - **Velocity (u)**: High-speed flow is concentrated over the initial upper surface, but there is clear evidence of flow instability or a thin wake behind the extremely slender trailing edge.
    - **Velocity (v)**: There is very little vertical displacement of the flow, indicating that the design is generating minimal lift despite its length.

- **Mapping & Key Changes**:
    - Design 1's high reward (1.18) comes from its extreme chord (r0=1.0, r2=1.0), but its geometric flaws (waisting) are caused by side radii being too low (r1=0.18, r3=0.21).
