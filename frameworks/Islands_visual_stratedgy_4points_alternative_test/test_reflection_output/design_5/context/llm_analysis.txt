**Geometry Observations:**
* The airfoil is extremely blunt and thick, resembling a rounded rectangular "slug" or block rather than a streamlined wing.
* The Leading Edge (LE) is essentially a flat vertical face, which causes massive drag.
* The Trailing Edge (TE) has a slight point, but the overall aspect ratio is nearly 1:1, meaning the chord length is roughly equal to the thickness.
* There is a slight "waist" or inward curvature on the lower surface, suggesting some points have lower radii, but not enough to create a slender profile.

**Flow Field Observations:**
* There is a massive stagnation point (high pressure, red) across the entire front face of the design.
* Flow separation occurs almost immediately at the "corners" of the front face, leading to a huge, low-velocity wake (blue) behind the object.
* The streamlines are deflected sharply away from the body, indicating zero aerodynamic efficiency and extremely high pressure drag.
* There is no visible circulation or lift-generating pressure differential between the upper and lower surfaces.

**Design 1 Mapping & Flaws:**
* **Radius Overload:** Design 1 used very high `radius_param` values for almost all points (r0=0.99, r3=0.98, r4=0.99). This pushed all control points to the outer boundary, creating a circle/square rather than a stretched airfoil.
* **Blunt LE:** Point CP3 (the LE) had a high radius (0.98) and its neighbors (CP2, CP4) were also pushed far out, preventing the formation of a sharp or even rounded nose.
* **Lack of Chord Stretching:** The `angle_param` values were distributed such that the design didn't "stretch" across the x-axis.

**Key Changes Needed:**
1. **Pinch the Mid-body:** Drastically reduce the `radius_param` for CP1, CP2, CP4, CP5, and CP6 (to values < 0.3) to "collapse" the thickness and create a high aspect ratio.
2. **Sharpen the Nose:** While keeping the LE (CP3) at a high radius, ensure its neighboring angles are very close to 0.0 to create a protruding "beak" rather than a flat face.
3. **Enforce Extreme Smoothness:** Set `edgy_param` to -1.0 for all non-TE points to eliminate the "blocky" corners seen in Design 1.
