To address the lack of lift, I will introduce camber to the airfoil by adjusting the angle parameters. I will keep the radius parameters at 1.0 to maintain the overall size, and the edgy parameters at 0.0 for smooth curves. The angle parameters will be set to introduce a positive camber. Specifically, I will set a positive angle for the second control point and a negative angle for the third control point, relative to the first and fourth control points. This should create an upward curve on the upper surface and a relatively flat lower surface.
