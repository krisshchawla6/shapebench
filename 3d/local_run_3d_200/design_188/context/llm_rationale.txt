*   **Strategy Focus: Exploration of UNDEREXPLORED regions:** The most significant unexplored region is the use of `NACA 00xx` airfoils (zero camber). All top designs use cambered airfoils (24xx or 44xx). This design will boldly explore a symmetric airfoil section.
*   **Combine zero camber with moderate-to-high sweep:** To compensate for the lack of camber-induced lift, a higher sweep angle than Design 1 (`le_sweep=65.5`) will be chosen, around `72.0` degrees. This higher sweep should enhance vortex lift, which will be critical for a symmetric airfoil. This explores the mid-to-high sweep range that performed poorly in Design 6 (75.6 deg), but with different airfoil and twist.
*   **Implement aggressive washout with the symmetric airfoil:** To ensure good lift distribution and control induced drag for a symmetric, highly-swept wing, a significant amount of washout will be applied. `twist_root` will be moderately negative (`-8.0`), and `twist_tip` will be pushed more aggressively negative (`-9.5`), close to its limit, to ensure proper load relief towards the tip.
*   **Investigate a thicker symmetric airfoil:** Design 1 was 12% thick, Design 6 was 7% (low reward). A `naca_t=18` will be used. This explores the higher end of the thickness range (6-24) in combination with a symmetric profile, which is an unexplored combination. It provides more volume and potential for structural benefits, while testing its aerodynamic impact.
*   **Slightly positive dihedral:** Design 1 was slightly negative (-1.72). Most designs are negative. Design 5 explored positive dihedral (7.57) but with low reward. This design will explore a moderate positive `dihedral` (`5.0`), testing its interaction with the high sweep and aggressive washout for lateral stability without being extreme.
*   **Moderate root chord:** The `root_chord_in` will be set to `38.0` inches, slightly larger than Design 1. This keeps the wing within a reasonable scale while being on the larger side of the commonly explored range, potentially offering more surface area for lift generation with the new profile.
