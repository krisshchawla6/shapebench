To maximize lift generation and potentially boost vortex strength, I will specify `naca_m=4` and `naca_p=4`, leveraging the full camber capability available.

Building on the success of washout in Design 1, I will set a `twist_root` of `-9.5` degrees and a `twist_tip` of `-3.0` degrees. This creates a substantial washout of 6.5 degrees, intended to shed load efficiently towards the wing root, delay tip stall, and reduce induced drag.
