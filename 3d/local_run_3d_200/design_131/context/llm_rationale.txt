*   **Exploitation of Design 1's Success:** Design 1 achieved an exceptionally high reward, primarily due to its well-balanced parameters. The new design aims to refine these successful characteristics rather than radically deviate.
*   **Optimal Sweep Range:** The most successful designs cluster in the 63-66 degree sweep range. Setting `le_sweep` to 64.8 degrees keeps it within this proven optimal range, slightly below Design 1, aiming to reduce induced drag while maintaining strong vortex generation.
*   **Aggressive Washout Twist:** High-reward designs consistently feature significant washout. By setting `twist_root` to -9.5 degrees (closer to the limit) and `twist_tip` to -4.0 degrees, we create a strong washout (5.5 degrees difference), which should further improve spanwise lift distribution and delay tip stall, crucial for maximizing CL/CDi.
*   **Cambered Airfoil with Reduced Thickness:** Design 1 used NACA 4412. We will maintain `naca_m=4` and `naca_p=4` for strong lift characteristics. Reducing `naca_t` to 10% (from Design 1's 12%) aims to slightly reduce profile drag, a strategy supported by Design 2 having `naca_t=11`.
*   **Increased Anhedral:** The top three designs all exhibit anhedral. Increasing `dihedral` to -3.0 degrees (more negative than Design 1's -1.72) is a logical step to further explore the benefits of anhedral for lateral stability and potentially vortex stability in delta wings.
*   **Moderate Root Chord:** Keeping `root_chord_in` at 33.5 inches maintains the overall scale within the successful range observed in Design 1 and Design 2, avoiding the larger chords that led to lower rewards in other designs.
