*   **Hybridization Strategy**: This new design is a hybrid drawing inspiration from the top-performing Design 2 (Reward: 4016.0090, NACA 4412, moderate sweep and twist) and Design 3 (Reward: 284.3274, higher sweep, positive dihedral, thinner NACA 2406), aiming to combine their respective strengths for enhanced CL/CDi.
*   **Increased Sweep for Vortex Lift**: Adopting a `le_sweep` of 68.0 degrees, intermediate between Design 2's 65.5 and Design 3's 68.9, aims to promote stronger and more stable leading-edge vortices, a key contributor to lift for delta wings, while avoiding the potentially detrimental effects of very high sweep seen in lower-ranked designs.
*   **Optimized Twist Distribution**: We are retaining a substantial negative `twist_root` (-7.5 degrees) similar to Design 2 to prevent root stall, but increasing the `twist_tip` washout to -7.0 degrees. This provides a more balanced and aggressive washout towards the tip, resembling Design 3's tip twist (-7.9) more than Design 2's (-4.6), which should improve spanwise load distribution and reduce induced drag.
*   **High Camber, Reduced Thickness Airfoil**: Leveraging the highly successful `naca_m=4` and `naca_p=4` from Design 2 for superior lift generation, we will pair it with a slightly reduced `naca_t=10`. This thickness is thinner than Design 2's 12 but thicker than Design 3's 6, aiming for a balance of lower profile drag and structural viability.
*   **Exploration of Positive Dihedral**: Moving away from the negative dihedral common in many designs, we will incorporate a positive `dihedral` of 7.0 degrees, similar to Design 3. This explores the effect of positive dihedral on lateral stability and potential interactions with vortex flow patterns, representing a novel design point in this parameter.
*   **Moderate Root Chord**: The `root_chord_in` is set to 36.0 inches, slightly larger than Design 2's 34.78 and Design 3's 33.97. This ensures a sufficient reference area for lift while remaining within a practical scale, allowing for adequate wing volume for the chosen airfoil and contributing to overall performance scaling.
