*   **Exploration of Extreme Thinness (`naca_t=6`):** The current population designs mainly use `naca_t` between 9 and 18. Pushing to the lower boundary of 6% thickness, while maintaining beneficial camber, is a significant exploration. This could drastically reduce drag due to a smaller profile area, potentially leading to a higher CL/CDi if lift generation remains efficient.
*   **Targeting Higher Sweep with Camber (`le_sweep=70.0`):** While high sweep angles (70+) have yielded low rewards previously, those designs exclusively used symmetric airfoils. This new design explores a moderately high sweep (70.0 degrees) in combination with a proven cambered airfoil (NACA 44xx) to properly assess the aerodynamic benefits of increased vortex lift in this region without the penalty of a non-lifting profile.
*   **Maximum Washout Profile (`twist_root=-10.0`, `twist_tip=-10.0`):** Top-performing designs already feature significant washout. This design pushes to the maximum permissible washout at both the root and tip. This aggressive twist distribution aims to further alleviate tip loading, which can mitigate induced drag and potentially delay tip stall, crucial for maximizing efficiency on a delta wing.
*   **Exploring Moderate Positive Dihedral (`dihedral=7.5`):** The performance of positive dihedral has not been clearly assessed in the context of high-reward designs. Previous designs with positive dihedral used symmetric airfoils and performed poorly. By integrating a moderate positive dihedral (7.5 degrees) with a cambered, thin, and highly swept wing, we aim to understand its impact on lateral stability and induced drag characteristics more accurately, exploring a new design strategy.
*   **Scaled Root Chord (`root_chord_in=25.0`):** The top designs have varied root chords (33-41 inches). Exploring a slightly smaller root chord (25.0 inches) for this novel combination of parameters will investigate the scaling effects of this particular geometry, allowing us to see if the aerodynamic benefits hold across different overall wing sizes.
