*   **Hybrid Strategy - Core Philosophy**: Our approach is to blend the most successful characteristics identified in the top three designs (Design 1, 2, and 3) to create a novel configuration. We will primarily leverage the high-performing sweep and twist distribution from Design 1, but integrate the camber strategy (`naca_p=0`) from Design 3, and explore an intermediate thickness.
*   **Leveraging Optimal Sweep**: Design 1 and Design 3 both had `le_sweep` around 65.5-65.6 degrees, indicating this sweep angle is highly effective for CL/CDi. The new design will maintain this successful characteristic, setting `le_sweep` to 66.0 to slightly probe the local optimum.
*   **Exploring Camber Position**: While Design 1's NACA 4412 (`naca_p=4`) was highly successful, Design 3 (NACA 4016, `naca_p=0`) also achieved a respectable reward. The new design will utilize `naca_m=4` (high camber) but set `naca_p=0` (max camber at root) to investigate if this camber distribution can further enhance lift characteristics or shift the sweet spot for CL/CDi when combined with other optimal parameters. An intermediate `naca_t=14` will be used to explore a balance between the thinner 12 (D1) and thicker 16 (D3) airfoils.
*   **Refined Twist Distribution**: Design 1's moderate washout (`twist_root=-7.1`, `twist_tip=-4.6`) was key. We will slightly modify this to `twist_root=-8.5` and `twist_tip=-6.5`. This maintains a beneficial washout but with a slightly larger difference between root and tip, aiming to further optimize spanwise load distribution and potentially manage vortex stability across a wider operating range.
*   **Root Chord Adjustment**: Design 1 had `root_chord_in=34.786`, while Design 3 had `40.868`. The new design will use `root_chord_in=37.5`. This explores a slightly larger wing area than Design 1, which might lead to higher absolute lift, without the potentially diminishing returns or increased induced drag observed with the very large chord of Design 3.
*   **Dihedral Consistency**: Maintain `dihedral` close to the slightly negative value of Design 1 (`-1.7`), setting it to `-2.5`. This aligns with the observed pattern that slightly negative dihedral correlates with the higher reward designs, likely contributing to stability without hindering CL/CDi.
