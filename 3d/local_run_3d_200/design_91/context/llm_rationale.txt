*   The overwhelming success of Design 1 (4016.0090 reward) dictates an exploitation strategy. We must analyze its parameters to replicate and refine its high-performing characteristics.
*   Design 1 utilizes a NACA 4412 airfoil. This confirms that positive camber (`naca_m=4`, `naca_p=4`) is crucial for maximizing CL/CDi, as pure symmetric airfoils (NACA 00xx in Designs 5 & 6) result in significantly lower rewards. We will retain this camber.
*   The twist distribution of Design 1 (`twist_root=-7.1`, `twist_tip=-4.6`) is less aggressive than several lower-performing designs. This milder twist, particularly at the tip, likely contributes to a more efficient lift distribution. The new design will explore a slightly less negative twist at both root and tip to further optimize lift efficiency and reduce induced drag.
*   The leading edge sweep of 65.5 degrees and root chord of 34.7 inches in Design 1 represent a sweet spot for balancing vortex lift and induced drag. The new design will slightly perturb these values to fine-tune the planform geometry.
*   Design 1 features a NACA `naca_t=12` (12% thickness). To further refine the airfoil and potentially reduce form drag, the new design will explore a slightly thinner profile with `naca_t=10`, while maintaining the successful camber.
*   The dihedral of Design 1 is very slightly negative (-1.7 degrees). We will maintain a small negative dihedral (-2.0 degrees) to stay within the range of successful designs.
