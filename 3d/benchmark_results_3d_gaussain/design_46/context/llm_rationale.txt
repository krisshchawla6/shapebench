*   **Explore High Sweep Boundary:** Pushing `le_sweep` to `75.0` will allow us to investigate the performance implications of an even higher sweep angle, testing the limits of vortex lift enhancement while potentially incurring higher induced drag. This moves beyond the 71-degree sweet spot observed.
*   **Aggressive Differential Twist:** Introduce a novel twist distribution by setting `twist_root` to `2.0` (positive wash-in) and `twist_tip` to `-10.0` (maximum washout). This creates a strongly twisted wing that redistributes lift more towards the root while aggressively unloading the tip, aiming to generate a unique vortex interaction and load profile.
*   **Maximize Anhedral:** Maintain the extreme `dihedral` of `-15.0` as this value consistently appears in high-reward designs (like Design 1) and warrants further exploration in conjunction with the new twist strategy.
*   **Reduced Root Chord Exploration:** Set `root_chord_in` to `25.0` to test a more compact wing planform. This is a significant reduction from the current successful range (30-33 inches) and will evaluate if a smaller overall size can improve CL/CDi by reducing wetted area or aspect ratio effects.
*   **Camber Profile Diversification:** Select a `NACA 4409` airfoil. While a `NACA 4413` performed poorly in Design 3, this design will test the `NACA 44xx` camber profile (higher camber, further aft peak) with a *thinner* section (09 instead of 13) and in combination with the new high-performing sweep, twist, and dihedral parameters. This explores if the `44xx` camber can be beneficial under different conditions.
