I will explore a specific washout gradient: aggressive at the root (`twist_root=-9.0`) but slightly less so at the tip (`twist_tip=-8.0`). This creates a new aggressive washout profile to observe its effect on lift distribution and induced drag.

This explores the possibility of achieving high CL/CDi with a more compact, potentially lighter design, thus improving overall efficiency.
