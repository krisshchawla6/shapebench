*   The current leading designs exhibit extreme parameter values (high sweep, max washout, max anhedral). While successful, this indicates a potential local optimum. To avoid premature convergence and explore the design space more thoroughly, a novel design should explore a different region.
*   Previous high-reward designs leaned heavily on very high sweep (>70 deg). I will explore a more moderate sweep (`62.5` degrees). This moves away from the extremes but is still firmly in the delta wing regime, aiming for a balance between vortex lift and potentially more distributed lift generation. This is a significant departure from the top performers (70-77 deg) but avoids the very poor performance of the lowest sweep (59 deg).
*   Washout (negative twist) has shown to be critical for efficient lift distribution across all successful designs. I will maintain significant washout, applying a large negative twist at the tip (`-9.5` degrees) to unload it, and a moderate negative twist at the root (`-6.0` degrees) to ensure a controlled spanwise load profile, which is a different distribution than the previous population.
*   Cambered airfoils were present in lower-performing designs, often with specific sweep or dihedral choices. I will explicitly test a cambered airfoil (`naca_m=4`, `naca_p=4`) to explore if it can improve CL/CDi when paired with a moderate sweep and optimized twist, as opposed to the symmetric airfoils in the top designs.
*   All top designs used extreme anhedral (`dihedral=-15.0`). This might be crucial, but it's also a strong constraint. I will make a bold exploratory move by setting `dihedral=0.0` (neutral dihedral) to understand its impact on stability and the CL/CDi ratio without the added complexity of anhedral. This is a significant departure from current trends and helps explore a novel region.
*   I will select a root chord in the mid-range (`35.0` inches) to provide adequate wing area without making the wing excessively large, balancing physical dimensions with the chosen aerodynamic parameters. I will also choose an intermediate thickness (`naca_t=12`) for the cambered airfoil to provide structural integrity while keeping drag reasonable.
