*   **Strategy Focus: Novel Exploration**: The top designs consistently feature high sweep, significant washout, and anhedral, indicating a robust design regime. This new design aims to push the boundaries of these successful characteristics while introducing a novel camber profile to further enhance performance and explore a different part of the design space.
*   **Leveraging High Sweep**: High-reward designs consistently feature leading-edge sweeps around 70-73 degrees. To enhance vortex lift generation and explore beyond the existing cluster, this design increases `le_sweep` to 75.0 degrees, pushing towards the upper limit of the range.
*   **Optimizing Scale**: High-reward designs use a larger `root_chord_in` (around 32-35 inches). This design opts for a slightly larger `root_chord_in` of 38.0 inches, exploring the effect of increased scale on lift generation while maintaining the high sweep characteristics.
*   **Refined Washout Distribution**: Maximum washout at the tip (`twist_tip = -10.0`) is a strong indicator of high performance in the database. This is retained to ensure stable tip vortices and good load distribution. A moderate `twist_root` of -5.0 degrees is chosen to explore a specific washout gradient, aiming to fine-tune the spanwise lift distribution.
*   **Aggressive Anhedral**: Strong negative `dihedral` (anhedral) has been highly correlated with success (e.g., -15.0 deg in Design 2). This design incorporates a significant `dihedral` of -12.0 degrees, which is expected to contribute to positive lateral stability characteristics, particularly at high angles of attack through vortex interaction.
*   **Novel Camber Exploration**: While NACA 24xx airfoils are prevalent in top designs, this design explores a NACA 44xx profile (`naca_m=4`, `naca_p=4`). This aims to provide higher inherent camber (4% at 40% chord) compared to NACA 24xx (2% at 40% chord), potentially increasing CL_alpha and enabling higher lift coefficients at lower angles of attack, which could reduce induced drag for a given lift requirement. The `naca_t` is set to 9, a moderate thickness to balance structural considerations with reduced profile drag.
