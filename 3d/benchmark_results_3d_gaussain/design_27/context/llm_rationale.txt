*   **Hybrid Blend for Success:** Our new design will blend the highly successful aggressive washout (`twist_tip`) and strong anhedral (`dihedral`) from Design 1, which appear to be crucial for its performance, with a slightly reduced sweep and a varied NACA thickness observed in other top performers (Design 2, 4).
*   **Sweep Optimization:** Design 1 has a very high `le_sweep` of 71.23. Design 2, also high performing, is at 69.77. We will choose a value of 70.0 degrees to explore a point slightly below Design 1 but still within the highly effective sweep range, aiming for an optimal balance of vortex lift and reduced induced drag.
*   **Persistent Washout:** The aggressive `twist_tip=-10.0` has been consistently used in top designs (1 & 2) indicating its strong positive impact. We will maintain this maximum tip washout. For `twist_root`, we will choose a value of -9.0, keeping it strongly negative but slightly less extreme than Design 1's -10.0, to investigate if a subtle variation in root washout can fine-tune the load distribution.
*   **Anhedral Reinforcement:** Strong negative `dihedral` (anhedral) is a common characteristic among top designs. Design 1 has -15.0 and Design 2 has -11.66. We will set `dihedral` to -13.0, continuing the trend of substantial anhedral to influence stability and potentially aid vortex development.
*   **NACA Camber & Thickness Exploration:** The NACA 24xx series has proven effective. We will retain `naca_m=2` and `naca_p=4` for reliable camber. For `naca_t`, Design 1 used 7, Design 2 used 8, and Design 4 used 11. We will set `naca_t=10`, exploring a slightly thicker profile than the top design (2407) but still within a reasonable range, to evaluate its interaction with the selected sweep and twist.
*   **Moderate Root Chord:** The `root_chord_in` values for top designs are around 30-33 inches. We will choose 30.5 inches, a slightly smaller yet still substantial chord, to balance overall wing area with potential efficiency gains from reduced size.
