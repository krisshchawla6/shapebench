*   **Exploitation of Top Performers:** The current highest-reward design (Design 1) and other strong contenders heavily feature very high sweep, significant washout, and maximum negative dihedral. This new design will exploit these identified optimal parameter regimes to maximize CL/CDi.
*   **Maximize Leading-Edge Sweep:** Design 1 (77.84 deg) and Design 5 (79.86 deg) demonstrate that extremely high sweep is beneficial. The new design will push the sweep further towards the upper limit at 79.0 degrees to enhance vortex lift generation and stability.
*   **Apply Maximum Washout:** Both Design 1 (-9.55/-9.13 deg) and Design 2 (-10.0/-10.0 deg) show strong washout. To optimize lift distribution and reduce induced drag, the new design will utilize the maximum allowed negative twist for both root and tip: -10.0 degrees.
*   **Apply Maximum Negative Dihedral:** The consistent presence of -15.0 degrees dihedral in the top two designs strongly suggests its effectiveness. This parameter will be set to its maximum negative value to leverage its potential benefits for aerodynamic performance.

This design will maintain a symmetric profile (NACA 00) and explore a slightly increased thickness of `naca_t=7` to see if there's a small plateau or marginal improvement near the optimal thickness of 6, without significantly increasing profile drag.
