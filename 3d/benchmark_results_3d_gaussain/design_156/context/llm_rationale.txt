*   **Optimize lift distribution with strategic washout**: Aggressive negative twist (washout) is crucial for managing spanwise lift distribution and reducing induced drag. Instead of a constant washout, I will implement a slightly differentiated washout: maximum at the root (`twist_root = -10.0`) and slightly less aggressive but still significant at the tip (`twist_tip = -8.0`). This aims to reduce root loading, prevent premature root stall, and maintain effective lift towards the tips without over-penalizing the outer wing.

This strong correlation indicates that anhedral is highly beneficial for the objective function, likely by improving vortex stability, reducing effective span loading, or optimizing lateral stability characteristics for high-performance flight. I will maintain this max anhedral.
