Our strategy is to hybridize the characteristics of Design 1 (highest reward, unique constant negative twist) and Design 2 (second highest reward, strong tip washout with similar airfoil and sweep). This approach aims to combine their complementary strengths.

We will maintain this high sweep, setting it to `72.5` to explore slightly higher values in the proven range, and keep the `root_chord_in` at `33.5` inches to stay within the established effective scale.
*   **Hybrid Twist Strategy:** Design 1 used a constant `twist_root = -10.0` and `twist_tip = -10.0`. Design 2 used `twist_root = -6.10` and `twist_tip = -10.0`. We will combine these by maintaining `twist_tip = -10.0` (max negative, consistently high-performing), but setting `twist_root = -7.5`. This introduces a clear washout gradient (`-7.5` at root to `-10.0` at tip) while keeping the overall twist very negative, blending the uniform negative incidence of D1 with the strong tip washout of D2.
*   **Maximize Anhedral:** Design 1 utilized the maximum possible anhedral (`dihedral=-15.0`), which appears strongly correlated with high rewards. We will also set `dihedral=-15.0` to leverage this consistent trend.

We will maintain the `naca_m=2` and `naca_p=4` (cambered profile) but push to the absolute thinnest allowable `naca_t=6` to minimize profile drag and further explore its impact on CL/CDi.
*   **Explore Novel Combination:** This design represents a novel blend of an aggressive overall negative twist, a distinct washout gradient towards the tip, maximum anhedral, and the thinnest possible cambered airfoil.
