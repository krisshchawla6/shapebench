* **Strategic Exploration**: Instead of minor tweaks to Design 2, this design explores a "fuller" chord distribution by increasing the radii of the mid-section control points (CP1, CP2), moving away from the thin-profile regime of the current population.
* **Leading Edge Robustness**: CP0 is set with a very low `edgy_param` (-0.8841) and a higher radius (0.8542) to create a larger, blunter nose. This aims to reduce the magnitude of the initial suction peak and make the airfoil less sensitive to flow angles.
* **Camber Distribution**: By positioning CP1 and CP2 with distinct angular offsets (0.5231 and -0.2847), I am attempting to create a more efficient "S-shape" or supercritical-style lower surface to control the pressure gradient.
* **Smoothing Strategy**: Following the trend where high rewards (Designs 1 & 2) correlate with highly negative `edgy_params` for the first three points, I am pushing these towards the limit (-0.95) to ensure maximum boundary layer stability.
* **Trailing Edge Definition**: A moderate positive `edgy_param` (0.3211) for CP3 is used to create a clean Kutta condition at the trailing edge without the extreme curvature that led to lower performance in Design 3.
