A symmetric airfoil might offer different drag characteristics or performance at specific angles of attack compared to cambered sections. This will involve setting `naca_m=0` and `naca_p=0`.

Anhedral (`dihedral < 0`) is an unexplored region that could affect lateral stability and potentially interact differently with the flow field, especially when combined with high sweep. I will try a moderate anhedral value.
