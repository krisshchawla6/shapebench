Following the **Exploitation Strategy**, I will leverage the patterns observed in the highest-reward designs (Design 1, 2, 4, 3, 5) to refine and maximize the reward. The strategy is to push successful parameters to their proven optimal or extreme beneficial values.

*   The most dominant pattern in the top-performing designs is the **maximum washout** configuration, with `twist_root` at 10.0 and `twist_tip` at -10.0. This significantly contributes to an efficient lift distribution and reduced induced drag, which is paramount for the CL/CDi objective. This will be a core feature of the new design.
*   The high-reward designs cluster at **lower sweep angles**, specifically in the 45-50 degree range. I will choose a sweep of 47.5 degrees, slightly above the minimum, to maintain the benefits of lower induced drag and possibly explore a slightly different vortex behavior compared to the absolute minimum sweep of Design 1, while staying within the proven high-performance zone.
*   The use of **cambered NACA 24xx airfoils** is universal among the top designs. I will retain `naca_m=2` and `naca_p=4`. For `naca_t`, Design 1 used 09. Design 6 used 08, and while its overall reward was lower, the airfoil thickness itself is a variable worth exploring for drag reduction. I will select `naca_t=8` to slightly reduce thickness compared to Design 1, aiming for a minor drag benefit while maintaining a good camber profile.
*   Several top designs, particularly Design 3 and Design 5, show strong performance with **maximum positive dihedral (15.0 degrees)**. While Design 1 had 7.34 degrees, pushing the dihedral to its maximum value (15.0) will allow us to fully explore its impact on stability and potentially favorable interaction with the highly twisted wing for improved CL/CDi.
*   The `root_chord_in` varied across top designs without a clear optimum. I will select a value of 31.5 inches, which is within the range of top performers (25-33 inches) and offers a good size without being excessively large or small, allowing the other optimized parameters to express their full potential.
