*   **Bold Sweep Exploration (60.0 deg)**: The current top designs are clustered at low sweeps (46-48 deg). To diversify and creatively explore the design space, I will significantly increase the leading-edge sweep to 60.0 degrees. This moves into a regime where vortex lift plays a more dominant role, potentially uncovering novel high-performance solutions if combined effectively with other parameters, even though Design 6 with 55.6 degrees performed poorly, indicating that sweep is not the only factor.
*   **Novel Airfoil Strategy (NACA 0010 - Symmetric)**: All existing high-reward designs utilize cambered NACA 24xx airfoils. To introduce fundamental diversity, I will specify a symmetric NACA 0010 airfoil (m=0, p=0). This investigates whether high CL/CDi can be achieved through pure planform and twist optimization without reliance on camber, simplifying potential manufacturing and exploring a different aerodynamic strategy.
*   **Maintain Aggressive Washout (-10.0 deg tip twist, 9.0 deg root twist)**: The consistent trend among high-reward designs is a significant positive root twist paired with maximum negative tip twist (washout). This suggests effective load distribution control across the span, crucial for reducing induced drag. I will maintain this aggressive twist strategy as a proven performance enhancer to complement the novel sweep and airfoil choices.
*   **Explore Negative Dihedral (-8.0 deg)**: All top designs exhibit high positive dihedral. To broaden the exploration and challenge existing patterns, I will introduce a moderately negative dihedral of -8.0 degrees. This tests its effect on lateral stability and induced drag in conjunction with a high-sweep, symmetric wing, potentially revealing new optimal trade-offs.
*   **Moderate Root Chord and Thickness (32.0 in, 10%)**: I will set the root chord to a moderate 32.0 inches and the thickness to 10% (NACA 0010). This keeps these parameters within a reasonable, non-extreme range, allowing the primary focus to remain on the bolder explorations of sweep, airfoil type, twist, and dihedral.
