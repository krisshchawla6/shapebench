*   **Apply the Exploration Strategy by testing high sweep:** Most successful designs cluster around 65 degrees sweep. Design 5 at 75.6 degrees showed moderate performance, indicating potential for higher sweep. I will push towards the upper boundary, setting `le_sweep` to 78.0 degrees, to explore the very high sweep regime more thoroughly.
*   **Introduce positive root twist (washin):** All previous designs utilized negative twist (washout) at both root and tip. This is a significantly *under-explored* region of the twist parameter space. I will set `twist_root` to +3.0 degrees, combined with a mild negative `twist_tip` of -2.0 degrees, to observe the aerodynamic effects of washin and a different twist distribution.
*   **Explore positive dihedral:** Dihedral has varied, with Design 1 being slightly negative and Design 2 being moderately positive, and Design 5 extremely negative. To explore the positive dihedral range more, I will select `dihedral` at 7.5 degrees, representing a moderate positive angle, to understand its interaction with high sweep and the new twist strategy.

To maintain a known good lift generation characteristic, I will use `naca_m=4` and `naca_p=4`. However, to test its interaction with higher sweep and a new twist, I will slightly reduce `naca_t` to 10, exploring a slightly thinner profile than Design 1's 12.
*   **Keep root chord moderate:** The `root_chord_in` values have not shown a strong correlation with relative reward, as it scales the wing. I will keep it in a moderate range, similar to previous designs (e.g., 35.0 inches), to focus the exploration on the shape-defining parameters.
