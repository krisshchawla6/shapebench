We will increase the `root_chord_in` to 37.0 inches, aiming for a larger wing area to boost overall lift capability without becoming excessively large or heavy, finding a mid-point between D1 and D3.
*   **Aggressive Root Twist for High Lift:** Designs 2 and 3 both utilize the maximum negative `twist_root` of -10.0°. We will adopt this aggressive root twist to promote strong lift generation at the root, potentially increasing the overall lift coefficient.

With a `twist_root` of -10.0°, we target a `twist_tip` of -7.5° to maintain a similar effective washout of -2.5 degrees, aiming to replicate Design 1's efficient lift distribution while leveraging the aggressive root twist.
