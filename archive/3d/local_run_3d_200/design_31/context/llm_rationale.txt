We will maintain `twist_root = -10.0` but implement a positive `twist_tip` (e.g., 5.0 degrees). This creates a strong washout condition relative to the root, aiming to unload the wing tips, potentially reducing induced drag and improving stall characteristics by distributing lift more inboard. This is a significant departure from previous designs.
