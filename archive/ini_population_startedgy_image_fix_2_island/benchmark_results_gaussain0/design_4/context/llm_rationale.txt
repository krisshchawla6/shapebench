*   **Camber/Lift (CP0 & CP2):** I am introducing asymmetry by setting a high positive `radius_param` for the upper control point (CP0) and a lower `radius_param` for the bottom (CP2). This creates a thickness gradient intended to produce a pressure differential (lift).
