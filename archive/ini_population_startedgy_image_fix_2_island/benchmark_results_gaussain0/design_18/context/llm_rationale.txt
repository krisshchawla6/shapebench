- **Strategy Focus (Exploration)**: The current population is heavily clustered around a specific "negative-radius CP0" strategy. I am moving into the underexplored region of **positive-radius CP0 coupled with extreme angular sweep**. This creates a sharp, swept leading edge rather than the blunt, humped leading edges seen in Designs 1-4.
- **Boundary Exploration**: I am pushing `angle_param` values for CP0 and CP3 to their near-limits (-0.95 and -0.88) to explore "hooked" or "crescent" geometries that have not been tested in the top-performing designs.
- **Edginess Diversity**: While previous successful designs favored smooth shapes (negative `edgy_param`), Design 6 showed that a sharp point (edgy=1.0) can still maintain a high rank. I am introducing a "hybrid" sharpness profile: a very smooth leading edge (CP0) with high-edginess internal and trailing points (CP1, CP3) to test if "faceted" geometric transitions can optimize pressure distribution.
- **Camber Inversion**: By setting r1 to a significant negative value and r2 to a positive value, I am attempting to create a high-camber profile that is the geometric inverse of the current population's bias, searching for a new local optimum in the design space.
