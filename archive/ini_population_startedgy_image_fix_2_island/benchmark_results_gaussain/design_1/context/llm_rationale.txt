*   **Leading Edge (CP 0):** Needs to be round and smooth to prevent leading-edge separation. I will set $r_0$ slightly positive for thickness and $e_0$ close to -1.0 (very smooth).

*   **Camber and Thickness (CP 1 & 3):** To generate lift, the top surface (CP 1) needs more curvature/volume than the bottom (CP 3). I will set $r_1 > r_3$. Both should remain relatively smooth ($e < 0$).
