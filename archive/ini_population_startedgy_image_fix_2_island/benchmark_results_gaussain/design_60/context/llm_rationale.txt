- **Strategy Focus - Visual Intuition & Diversity:** Instead of tweaking the existing "forward-loaded" shape of Design 1, this design attempts a **Laminar Flow / Aft-Loaded** strategy. This is a distinct aerodynamic philosophy that often yields higher efficiency (L/D) by delaying the transition to turbulent flow.
- **CP0 (Upper Fore):** Increased `angle_param` from ~0.09 to **0.18** and slightly reduced `radius_param` to **0.31** to push the initial curvature peak back and thin the leading edge slightly for lower drag.
- **CP1 (Upper Aft):** Pushed `angle_param` significantly aft to **0.58** (from 0.46) and reduced `radius_param` to **0.22**. This creates a "rooftop" pressure distribution that extends further back before recovery begins.
- **CP2 (Trailing Edge):** Maximized `radius_param` to **0.99** and `edgy_param` to **0.998** for the sharpest possible geometric release. The `angle_param` is adjusted to **-0.21** (less negative than Design 1's -0.25) to reduce the camber slightly at the tail, balancing the pitching moment.
- **CP3 (Lower Surface):** `radius_param` increased to **-0.08** (flatter than -0.13) and `angle_param` moved aft to **-0.35** to align with the upper surface shift, creating a cleaner, lower-drag profile.
