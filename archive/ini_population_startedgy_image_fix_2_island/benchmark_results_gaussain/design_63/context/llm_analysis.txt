- **Shape Profile:** The airfoil exhibits a moderate thickness distribution with a relatively blunt leading edge and a distinct, sharp trailing edge.
- **Camber Line:** There is visible positive camber, particularly in the aft section, suggesting an aft-loaded design strategy.

**Flow Field Analysis (Design 1):**

- **Velocity (1_u.png):** High-velocity region (red) correlates with the suction peak. The wake region (blue/green behind TE) appears somewhat thick, indicating a potential source of pressure drag.
- **Vertical Velocity (1_v.png):** There is significant downwash behind the airfoil, confirming lift generation, but the transition at the trailing edge shows some volatility, hinting at potential vorticity shedding or instability.
