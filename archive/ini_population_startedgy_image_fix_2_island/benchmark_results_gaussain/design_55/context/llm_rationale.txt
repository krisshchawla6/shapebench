*   **Geometric Strategy:**

    *   **P3 (Lower):** Use a slightly negative radius `~-0.05` to slim down the profile, reducing pressure drag on the lower side.
*   **Evolutionary Goal:** This design moves away from the "blunt nose" local optimum of Design 1 to explore a "laminar flow" style profile with a finer leading edge and smoother thickness distribution.
